TLSCONTACT TO INTRODUCE ONLINE IDENTITY VERIFICATION TO JOIN LIVE QUEUE FOR VISA APPOINTMENTS

17/10/2024

In October 2024 in Belarus, TLScontact will test an identity verification procedure to join the Live Queue for visa applications to Italy.

Why are we introducing this change?

This new measure will ensure better access to Live Queue registration for elderly and disabled applicants, as well as those living in remote areas.

How does the identity verification work?

The online identity verification is only an alternative security step to the Live Queue procedure, which remains unchanged. Registration for the Live Queue, including the verification process, is always free of charge.

Please read the instructions below:

1. Register for the ID verification process

  • To join the ‘live queue’, you will need to send us a request via our online contact form, with the subject ‘Verification’.
  • Please include the following information: full name according to passport bio page, date of birth, passport number (short with serial number, not ID card), email, phone number, and place of residence (town, region) according to your passport.
    NB: One application can only include the details of close relatives (parents and minor children, and grandparents if they are over 65 years of age).
  • Once received, your request will be added to the waiting list for processing. Depending on the number of applications, the waiting time can be up to 30 working days.
  • An identity verification request will not be considered if it does not contain the required data or is sent with the wrong subject.

2. Arrange your ID verification call

  • A TLScontact agent will call you from the TLScontact call centre to arrange a date and time for your identity verification video call. Please note if you miss the call your inquiry will be cancelled. You will have to send a new one.

3. Attend your ID verification call

  • On the agreed date for the video call, you will receive an email from TLScontact at the agreed time with a link for video communication. When you receive the email, please click on the link within the next 3 minutes. If you do not access the link as scheduled, your inquiry will be cancelled. You will be able to send only 1 more inquiry. After 2 cases of no-show at video-call you won’t be able to participate in online verification to obtain a live queue number.
  • You will need a laptop, tablet, or mobile phone with a video camera and speakers. Ensure you are in a quiet and bright place for the call, to ensure the best communication and visibility with our TLScontact agent. We recommend to use browsers: Chrome, Microsoft Edge or Safari.
  • The agent will call you and ask you to state your name and surname in front of the video camera and show the photo page of your passport. All adult family members listed on the application must be present during the video call session. Please note that the presence or other involvement of unauthorized persons during the verification process is prohibited and will result in the rejection of your request. An exception may be made for family members of elderly (over 65) and disabled applicants who require assistance.
  • The video identity verification will be considered unsuccessful due to slow internet speed, poor lighting, appearance not matching the passport photo, or any other element disrupting the recognition and verification of your identity.

4. Finalise your registration

  • If the verification is successful, you will receive an email from TLScontact with a number in the live queue.

Please note the following:

Applicants’ data obtained by TLScontact during the identity verification process will be processed in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

The identity verification process is experimental and does not apply to TLScontact's additional services. It is, however, subject to the basic provisions of our Terms of Service.

TLScontact does not record video of the verification process.

It is not possible to appeal against the result of the identity verification process.

The emails sent to you by TLScontact during the ID verification process do not require a written response and will not be processed. If you have any queries, please write to us using our Feedback & Complaints form, quoting your ITCS enquiry number for verification.

Citizens of the Russian Federation can apply for a visa through the Visa Centre upon presentation of a Residence Permit or a Temporary Residence Permit in the Republic of Belarus, issued at least 6 months before the trip to Italy and valid for at least 3 months after the applicant’s return to Belarus.

Changes to the package of documents for a visa

11/10/2024

The following documents are no longer required to apply for a visa:

  • Bus tickets or car documents for transfer from Minsk to the nearest airport;
  • Proof of residence in transit countries en route to Italy;
  • Documents confirming ownership of real estate and land in Belarus;
  • Sponsorship documents - in case of joint submission of documents for a visa with a sponsor on the same trip:
    a. Minors traveling with a sponsoring parent;
    b. Spouses who are traveling with a sponsoring spouse (with a valid marriage certificate). A "sponsorship statement" signed by the parent or spouse who will bear the costs is attached to the sponsored person's application package. Sponsorship documents are required if the sponsor is not traveling with a minor/spouse.

The following changes have also been made:

  • Submission of documents for a trip by car is possible only if its owner is also traveling with the applicant/is the applicant.
    Documents for the car for another person, a power of attorney from another individual or company issued to the applicant are not accepted.
  • Individual entrepreneurs provide the tax return for the last reporting period and only the first page of the cryptographic certificate.
  • In the bank statement, it is sufficient to indicate the account balance. A statement for the period can be provided to confirm your stay in Italy.

ADDITIONAL MATERIALS FOR VISA APPLICATION

The Embassy of the Italian Republic conducts selective monitoring of the intended use of issued visas. Obtaining an Italian Schengen visa stipulates that the main destination country is Italy (which does not exclude transit or stay in other Schengen countries).

  • Paid accommodation in Italy with names and dates;
  • Paid tickets to Italy or boarding pass;
  • Personal cheques for expenses in Italy (the amount of one is not less than EUR 50);
  • Bank statement showing expenses in Italy;

The above materials are not included in the mandatory package of documents, but may be useful for the decision to issue a visa. The maximum volume is up to 4 sheets.
